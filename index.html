🛠️ Who Are We?

Shree Raghavendra Tirtha Hitech Borewells and Power Drills is a trusted name in borewell drilling and groundwater solutions, serving residential, agricultural, and commercial needs with advanced technology and years of field expertise.

We specialize in:

  • ✅ Deep borewell drilling for homes, farms, apartments, and industries
  • ✅ Site inspection and borewell point selection by experienced geologists
  • ✅ High-pressure drilling rigs for all terrain types
  • ✅ Pump installation, maintenance, and servicing
  • ✅ Water yield testing and groundwater consultation

💧 How Can We Help You?

  • New Borewell Drilling: Precision drilling using advanced rigs for any soil type or depth
  • Site & Groundwater Surveys: Expert site inspection and scientific bore point selection
  • Pump Installation: Submersible and jet pump fitting with complete plumbing setup
  • Old Borewell Cleaning & Flushing: Restore water flow and remove blockages or silt
  • Borewell Extension: Deepen your existing borewell to reach new water sources
  • Emergency Services: Fast support for bore failure, motor issues, or dry borewells
